# Product Vision

## Purpose

Build a private storytelling journal that combines photos/videos and authored narrative into a coherent family archive with both curated and freeform discovery.

## Primary Users

- Author/admin: curates media, writes stories, manages taxonomy, publishes content.
- Family viewers: consume content primarily on mobile, then tablet/desktop.

## Product Principles

1. Narrative control via collections and manual ordering.
2. Organic discovery via dimensional and hierarchical tags.
3. Flexible card model: one schema, multiple display behaviors.
4. Consistent mobile-first interaction patterns.
5. Scalability toward large personal archives.

## In Scope (Current Product Direction)

- Story cards, gallery cards, question cards, quote cards, callout cards.
- Curated and freeform browsing.
- Tag-driven filtering across dimensions (Who/What/When/Where/Reflection/Collections). ??Collecitons??
- Admin workflows for cards, media, tags, questions, users, and themes. ??Collections??

## Out of Scope / Parked

- Multi-author archival model (identity, permissions, shared media lenses).
- Social-scale engagement features (likes/comments/share loops as primary product).
- Full production-grade video parity (parked until image + tagging flows stabilize).

## Success Signals

- Family viewers can navigate content naturally on mobile.
- Author can import and refine large media sets without workflow breakdown.
- Curated narrative and freeform exploration both feel coherent.
- Core admin flows remain reliable as data volume grows.
